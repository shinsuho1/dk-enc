Since its establishment in 2005, DKE&C Co., Ltd. has constantly carried out nuclear power, thermal power plant maintenance, and various industrial plant construction projects We have accumulated high technical skills and knowhow.

Based on the accumulated technical skills and systematic quality activities, the commissioning and maintenance works of Shin-Kori Units 3 and 4 and the Kori Nuclear Power Plant, By successfully carrying out various power generation maintenance projects such as the Boryeong Thermal Power Plant Recurring Maintenance Project, it has established itself as a small and medium-sized company in the power generation maintenance field. In addition, we are carrying out transmission and distribution projects with electric measuring equipment construction and mechanical equipment construction, and we are making our best efforts to diversify our business and grow into a global company in line with the trend of the times.

In 2023, we started anew with the mission of ‘DK E&C Co., Ltd.’ with the goal of becoming a global leader in the power generation and maintenance business. We promise to unite all executives and employees under the company motto of focusing on technology and securing competitiveness to become a global leader in the power generation and maintenance business. We will reward our customers’ trust with the best quality and technology.
