CEO Greetings

Hello, DK E&C Co., Ltd. customers.
Thank you for visiting our website. Since its establishment in 2005, DK E&C Co., Ltd. has been continuously carrying out maintenance work on nuclear power plants, thermal power plants, and various industrial plants. We have accumulated high technology and know-how.

Based on accumulated technology and systematic quality activities, we have completed the commissioning and maintenance work for Shin-Kori Units 3 and 4, the routine maintenance work for the Kori Nuclear Power Plant, By successfully carrying out various power generation maintenance projects, including Boryeong Thermal Power Plant routine maintenance work, we are establishing ourselves as a small power generation maintenance company. In addition, we are carrying out transmission and distribution projects, electrical work, and mechanical equipment construction, and are doing our best to expand our business areas in line with the trends of the times.

In 2023, we started anew with the mission of ‘DK E&C Co., Ltd.’ with the goal of becoming a global leader in the power generation and maintenance business. We promise to unite all executives and employees under the company motto of focusing on technology and securing competitiveness to become a global leader in the power generation and maintenance business. We will reward our customers’ trust with the best quality and technology.

Thank you again to all of you who consistently visit DK E&C Co., Ltd. We will do our best to grow together with our customers and become a company that is always trusted.
CEO Yoon Jeong-mi
