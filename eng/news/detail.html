The performance report session was held on this day with 20 people in attendance, including Suwon Special Mayor Lee Jae-jun, Second Vice Mayor Hwang In-guk, city council members, the Expo Promotion Advisory Committee, and experts.

‘Suwon Beauty Festa 2023’, held at the Suwon Convention Center on November 24-25 last year, is the first beauty fair held by a local government. Suwon Beauty Festa has 83 beauty products, 14 institutions and organizations participated, including healthcare companies and the Korea Cosmetic Industry Research Institute. In particular, product line companies such as cosmetics, skin, body, hair, and nails have beauty company pavilions, raw materials and beauty tech, Consulting-related companies participated in the cutting-edge beauty hall and attracted attention.

During the two days of the event, 4,541 people visited the fair and 203,972 people participated online. 962 of the on-site visitors In a satisfaction survey conducted on the target, 55.5% responded that they were very satisfied, 26.1% responded that they were somewhat satisfied, and 16.2% responded that they were moderately satisfied. Regarding re-participation next year When asked whether 94.9% responded that they would participate, 97.2% responded that they would like to continue hosting the event.
